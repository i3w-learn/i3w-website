The bet

Students don't lack content.
They lack daily help.

India's students have never had more to study. Notes, videos, test series, it's all a search away. What they don't have is someone to ask the moment they're stuck.

We use AI to be that someone: in Hinglish, on WhatsApp, for less than a samosa.

How we work

Startup speed,
institutional reach.

Ship fast

We ship in weeks, not quarters. A product in real hands teaches us more than any deck.

Listen daily

Beta calls, in-app behaviour, raw numbers. Students keep telling us what to build, and we keep following.

Distribute free

Infinity Learn already pulls 35 million views a month. We earn that attention first, then turn it into product.

Prove value

Every feature clears one bar: did a real student actually get a real win? If not, it doesn't ship.

Impact, not adjectives

Measured in outcomes, not adjectives.

We don't count features shipped. We count students who came back the next day, families who chose to pay, and children a model flagged in time.
