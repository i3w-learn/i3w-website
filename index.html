The bet

Students don't lack content.
They lack daily help.

India's students have more material than any generation before them. What they lack is someone in their corner at 11pm — in their own language, on their own phone, for the price of a samosa.

We close that gap with AI. Everything we build answers one question — did a real student get a real win today?
