Who we are

Small team.
An outsized swing.

We're a handful of people who've built learning products at national scale — at Unacademy, PhysicsWallah, Shopify, Atlassian, Zupee and NID.

I3W runs lean on purpose. Work is single-owner, end to end: the person who designs a thing ships it and watches the numbers afterwards. We build with the frontier, listen to students daily, and judge every feature on one question — did a real student get a real win today?
