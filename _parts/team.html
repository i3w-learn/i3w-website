Who we are

A small team, by design.

We're a handful of people who have built learning products at national scale, at Unacademy, PhysicsWallah, Shopify, Atlassian, Zupee and NID.

We stay lean on purpose. Work here is single owner, end to end: whoever designs a thing ships it and then watches the numbers. We build on the frontier, listen to students every day, and hold every feature to the question we ask everywhere: did a real student get a real win today?
