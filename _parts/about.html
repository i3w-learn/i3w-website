Our Story

Why we started in the hardest room we could find.

I3W, the Idea, Innovation and Impact Wing, is the AI unit inside Infinity Learn. We could have built AI for almost anything. We picked the one place where being wrong actually costs a student something, and started there.

The Bet

Content was never the problem.

A student today can find a hundred videos on any topic in seconds. None of them answer back when she is stuck at 11pm with no one to ask. That is the gap we build into: real help, in her own language, the moment she needs it.

Why Education, Why Now

We chose the hardest room.

Education gives AI no room to be wrong. A student under exam pressure, on a borrowed phone, switching between two languages mid-sentence, will forgive nothing. Build something that helps her, and you have built something that holds up anywhere.

Distribution we earn, not buy.

We don't buy attention. Infinity Learn already reaches 35 million students a month through its own channels, plus a ground network of 1,200+ Sri Chaitanya schools. Our job is to turn that reach into something a student actually keeps coming back to.
